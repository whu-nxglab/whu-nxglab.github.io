个人简介 Profile

牛晓光 教授 Prof. Xiaoguang Niu

武汉大学计算机学院，博士生导师 School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, PhD Supervisor

牛晓光教授是武汉大学计算机学院的资深教授，CCF、IEEE、ACM会员，并担任CCF物联网专委会委员及教育部创新团队成员。他的研究方向主要包括移动感知、智能物联网与空间智能，强化学习与具身智能，以及可信机器学习与智能体。

牛教授主持了多项重要科研项目，包括国家自然科学基金、国家重点研发计划项目子课题、湖北省自然科学基金、湖北省教改项目，以及教育部重点实验室开放基金。他作为研究骨干获得了中国测绘科技进步特等奖和北京市科技进步三等奖，并在国际期刊和会议上发表了81篇论文，其中期刊论文46篇。此外，他还担任多个国际会议和SCI期刊的审稿人。

Prof. Xiaoguang Niu is a distinguished professor at the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, and a member of CCF, IEEE, and ACM. He serves as a committee member of CCF IoT and a member of the Ministry of Education's innovation team. His research focuses on mobile sensing, intelligent IoT and spatial intelligence, reinforcement learning and embodied intelligence, and trustworthy machine learning and agents.

Prof. Niu has led numerous significant research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation, sub-projects of the National Key R&D Program, Hubei Provincial Natural Science Foundation, Hubei Provincial Education Reform Project, and the Ministry of Education Key Laboratory Open Fund. As a key researcher, he has received the China Surveying and Mapping Science and Technology Progress Special Award and the Beijing Science and Technology Progress Third Prize. He has published 81 papers in international journals and conferences, including 46 journal papers. Additionally, he serves as a reviewer for several international conferences and SCI journals.

研究方向 Research

移动感知、智能物联网与空间智能 Mobile Sensing, Intelligent IoT and Spatial Intelligence

面向真实应用场景，研究高精度移动感知、智能物联网系统、室内外定位与空间智能服务，构建可靠的感知与位置服务能力。 We study high-precision mobile sensing, intelligent IoT systems, indoor/outdoor localization, and spatial intelligence services to build reliable sensing and location-aware capabilities for real-world applications.

强化学习与具身智能 Reinforcement Learning and Embodied Intelligence

研究经验回放与面向物联网工业场景的智能决策方法，提升智能体在复杂任务中的学习效率与泛化能力。 We study experience replay and intelligent decision-making methods for industrial IoT scenarios to improve agent learning efficiency and generalization in complex tasks.

可信机器学习与智能体 Trustworthy Machine Learning and Agents

围绕不确定数据与开放任务，研究含噪标签学习、鲁棒优化、隐私保护学习与 LLM Agent，构建可靠的智能系统。 We study noisy-label learning, robust optimization, privacy-preserving learning, and LLM agents to build reliable intelligent systems for uncertain data and open-ended tasks.

招生信息 Recruitment Information

我们课题组长期招收博士生、硕士生和本科生加入研究团队，围绕移动感知、智能物联网与空间智能，强化学习与具身智能，以及可信机器学习与智能体等方向开展前沿研究，并为学生提供良好的科研环境和国际交流机会。 Our research group welcomes PhD, master's, and undergraduate students to join us. We conduct cutting-edge research in mobile sensing, intelligent IoT and spatial intelligence, reinforcement learning and embodied intelligence, and trustworthy machine learning and agents, while providing an excellent research environment and international exchange opportunities.

招生要求 Requirements

  • 具有扎实的计算机科学基础，对科研有浓厚兴趣 Solid foundation in computer science and strong interest in research
  • 具备良好的编程能力，熟悉至少一种编程语言（如C/C++、Python、Java等） Good programming skills, proficient in at least one programming language (e.g., C/C++, Python, Java)
  • 有较强的英语读写能力，能够阅读英文文献并撰写英文论文 Strong English reading and writing skills, able to read English literature and write papers in English
  • 有相关研究经验或发表过相关论文者优先 Priority given to those with relevant research experience or published papers
